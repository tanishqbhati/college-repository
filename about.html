About ZestyZen

Our mission to make healthy eating simple and delicious

At ZestyZen, we believe that healthy eating shouldn't be complicated or boring. Our mission is to provide a curated collection of nutritious, easy-to-make recipes that prove eating well can be both delicious and accessible. Whether you're a seasoned health enthusiast or just starting your wellness journey, ZestyZen is here to inspire you with recipes that nourish your body and delight your taste buds.

🥬

Fresh Ingredients

Every recipe uses whole, natural ingredients that you can find at your local market. No processed foods, no artificial additives — just real food.

⚖️

Balanced Nutrition

Each recipe comes with detailed nutrition information so you can make informed choices. We focus on balanced meals with the right mix of protein, carbs, and healthy fats.

👨‍🍳

Easy to Cook

Our recipes are designed for everyone — from beginners to experienced cooks. Simple steps, common ingredients, and no fancy equipment needed.

About This Project

ZestyZen was created as a web development project for the Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) program. The goal was to design and develop a modern, responsive, and user-friendly website that promotes healthy eating habits. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, this project demonstrates front-end web development skills including responsive design, dynamic content rendering, and interactive user interfaces.
